May 2014 Release Notes Updates

7/2/2014

In May 2014, GSA implemented changes to the eOffer/eMod System. You can read a detailed description of each update on the Release Notes website.

  • In situations where you would have needed to exercise a Novation Agreement Modification, a Name Change Modification, or an Address Change Modficiation, you may have also needed to update your DUNS number. Previously, you would have needed to reach out to your Contracting Officer, whereas now eMod gives you the option to modify your DUNS within the system.

    Learn more by clicking on the links for each respective Modification:

    • Novation Agreement Modification
    • Name Change Modification
    • Address Change Modficiation

The next release is in August of 2014. Check back to find out the latest updates.
