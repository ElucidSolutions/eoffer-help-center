August 2015 Release Notes Updates

08/24/2014

In August 2015, GSA implemented changes to the eOffer/eMod System. Pricing modifications were made to go into effect immediately during the May 2015 update. Previously, if a vendor submitted an instantly effective pricing modification along with a regularly processed modification, the modifications had to be manually separated. Now the instantly effective pricing modifications are automatically separated by the system. This goes into effect for the following modifications:

  • Temporary Price Reduction
  • Permanent Price Reduction (based on Most Favored Customer)
  • Permanent Price Reduction (Industry Partner Request)

  • Vendors may now respond to Solicitation Provisions in formatted text entry fields. Read about Responding to Solicitation Provisions here.

    • The next release is in November of 2015. Check back to find out the latest updates.
