November 2014 Release Notes Updates

10/28/2014

In November 2014, GSA implemented changes to the eOffer/eMod System. The follow changes have been made:

  • Vendors have been given the ability to express their Commercial Sales Practice (CSP) in terms of Markup/Build Out to better capture their business model.

  • When a vendor wishes to create a new offer based off a withdrawn or rejected offer, the system gives vendors the ability to import information from the previous eOffer. This feature now includes Commercial Sales Practice (CSP) data. Read about Commercial Sales Practices here.

The next release is in November of 2014. Check back to find out the latest updates.
